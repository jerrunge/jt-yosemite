What Echo brings, what you bring

A near-turnkey guided half day. Show up with clothes and sun protection, not a trunk full of gear.

All the technical gear for three anglers

Good to know

Park notes

Getting in

No timed-entry reservation needed in 2026. A standard $35 per vehicle entrance pass covers you. Have it ready to keep the gate quick.

Cold tents

Both nights are unheated canvas at elevation. Valley nights drop into the low 50s and Evergreen runs cooler. Pack a real warm layer for sleeping.

Bear country

Anything scented goes in the bear lockers at both properties. Nothing food-scented in the tent or visible in the car. This is enforced, not optional.

Weather

Mountain afternoons turn fast. Check the Yosemite Valley forecast the night before each day and pack the shell regardless. Full booking details live in your email confirmations.
